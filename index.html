About Me

I am a passionate software developer and hardware engineer with a deep understanding of the intersection between code and physical systems. My expertise spans a wide range of technologies, allowing me to design, build, and optimize both hardware and software solutions. I thrive on solving complex challenges and transforming innovative ideas into functional, high-quality products.

With a strong foundation in programming languages like Python, C++, and Java, as well as experience in embedded systems and microcontroller programming, I bridge the gap between software development and hardware engineering. I’ve worked on projects ranging from IoT devices to custom-built circuit boards, ensuring that both the hardware and software are optimized for performance, scalability, and user experience.

Driven by curiosity and a commitment to staying ahead of industry trends, I am constantly learning new tools and technologies to remain at the forefront of innovation. Whether I'm coding applications, developing embedded firmware, or working on system-level hardware design, I bring a holistic approach to engineering and problem-solving.
